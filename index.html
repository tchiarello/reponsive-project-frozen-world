Our differential is to take you to know the most beautiful and cold landscapes on our planet, with comfort and safety. Our packages are complete and offer an unforgettable experience for you. See below for our available destinations, and if you have any questions or suggestions, please contact us.

Siberia

The climate varies between the polar climate and the continental climate. In Siberia lies the coldest inhabited place in the world, the village of Oymyakon.

Greenland

Greenland is the largest island in the world and has over 44 000 km of coastline. The population is scarce, confined to small villages on the coast. The island has the second largest ice reserve in the world, only surpassed by Antarctica.

Antarctida

It is the coldest, driest continent, with the highest average altitude and highest winds in the world. The lowest temperature on Earth (-89.2 ° C) was recorded in Antarctica, with the average summer coast temperature being -10 ° C; inland, it is -40 ° C.
